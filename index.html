How much does marine litter effect the sea?

Marine litter has an effect on the sea, the sea animals eat the rubbish. On average, 100 millon sea animals die each year, it is possible that later there will be no sea animals, you may not really think that is does much but it does, it means no seafood either, so we need to stop people from chucking rubbish on the ground and to put the rubbish in the right place, the bin. 1.4 billon pounds of rubbish goes in the sea which is a lot especially over a period of time.
